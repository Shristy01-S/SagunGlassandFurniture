About Us

Your trusted source for quality furniture

Quality Craftsmanship Since Day One

At Sagun Glass & Furniture, we specialize in creating beautiful, durable wooden furniture that stands the test of time. Based in Nepal, we serve customers across the region with our commitment to quality and attention to detail, ensuring that every piece we create becomes a cherished part of your home.

We use only the finest wood materials and traditional Nepali woodworking techniques combined with modern design principles to create furniture that is both functional and beautiful.

Customization Available: We understand that every home is unique. That's why we offer complete customization services - clients can customize the design as per their requirements. Whether you need specific dimensions, colors, finishes, or design modifications, we work closely with you to bring your vision to life.
